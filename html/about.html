I'm an aspiring product manager with a technical and professional background.

At some point you figure out what you want to do with your career, hopefully. For me it came in 2017 while working with software companies to customise and integrate their products into existing business operations. I've always been a project manager and had always wanted to manage some kind of product development but it wasn't until then that I considered software products.

I built this site to teach myself the basics and enrolled in a coding bootcamp where I was finally able to express my passion for user-centric design where functionality and aesthetics are harnessed to deliver real human value.

I'm now doing exactly that at Skedulo and looking to move into a full-time product managment role.

Please  contact  me if you'd like to connect.
